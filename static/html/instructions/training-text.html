Training Instructions

Thank you for participating in this BrainGame. Before we will start the test, please read these test instructions carefully.


Page Browsing

In the following training phase you will be shown two different websites.

  1. Please take a close look at the websites and their loading behaviour.
  2. While looking at the pages, please make sure to click the links as fast as possible so that a red frame appears around them. Links are colored in blue and are underlined.
    The links are not supposed to open another webpage!


  3. You can look through the page for a total of 30 seconds or directly rate the page loading behaviour. A 30 second timer will automatically direct you to the next page once it is over.
  4. The rating button at the bottom of the page will be enabled and allows you to proceed to the page rating as soon as you like. Please rate the page loading behaviour.
