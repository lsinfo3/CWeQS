Thank you for participating in this BrainGame. Before we will start the test, please read these survey instructions carefully.


What is a BrainGame?

BrainGames use research techniques from cognitive science to better understand how our minds work when using the web. This understanding can improve web experiences by tailoring them around your contributed data.


Survey

Before participating in the actual BrainGame, we want you to fill up a survey regarding information about the participants. Filling out the survey is neccessary and helps us to evaluate the user behaviors more accurately.

The information you provide will be treated confidentially.


Please do NOT do any of the following things or you will be disqualified due to invalid test results:

  • Switch tabs or programs during the test
  • Zoom in or out during the test
  • Reload the page once you have begun the test
  • Activate an ad-blocker during the test
